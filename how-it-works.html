The Science of Electricity from Sun

Solar panels use something called the "photovoltaic effect." When sunlight hits special cells made of silicon, it knocks electrons loose, creating electricity. It's like sunlight giving electrons a push to move!

Fun fact: One hour of sunlight can power the whole world for a year!
